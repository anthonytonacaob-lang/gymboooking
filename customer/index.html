I. Reservation & Payment Terms

  • Lead Time: Reservations require at least seven (7) working days' notice for large events/tournaments, and one (1) working day for regular games.
  • Payment: Regular games require full payment to secure a slot. Large events require a 50% non-refundable downpayment to reserve the date.

II. Cancellation & Rescheduling

Rescheduling requests are subject to the following administrative fees based on the total booking amount:

  • 7+ days notice: 20% Fee
  • 1–3 days notice: 30% Fee
  • Same-day notice: 50% Fee
  • Force Majeure: In cases of typhoons, flooding, or government-mandated suspensions, bookings may be rescheduled or fully refunded without penalty.
